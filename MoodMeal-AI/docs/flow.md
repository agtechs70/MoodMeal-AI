# MoodMeal AI - Decision Flow

## Step 1: Welcome

MoodMeal AI greets the user.

Example:
"Hi! 👋 I'm MoodMeal AI.
I'll help you decide what to eat."

----------------------------------------

## Step 2: Decision Type

Question:

Do you want to:

1. 🍳 Cook at Home
2. 🛵 Order Food

----------------------------------------

## Step 3A: If User Chooses Cook

Ask:

- Veg or Non-Veg?
- Breakfast / Lunch / Dinner / Snack?
- Budget?
- Cooking Time?
- Mood?
- Health Goal?
- Available Ingredients? (Optional)

----------------------------------------

## Step 3B: If User Chooses Order

Ask:

- Veg or Non-Veg?
- Budget?
- Cuisine?
- Delivery or Dining?
- Mood?
- Healthy or Fast Food?

----------------------------------------

## Step 4

AI analyzes all answers.

----------------------------------------

## Step 5

AI decides the best meal.

----------------------------------------

## Step 6

AI explains WHY it selected that meal.

----------------------------------------

## Step 7

Provide:

- Meal Recommendation
- Nutrition
- Recipe OR Restaurant Suggestion
- Grocery List (if required)

----------------------------------------

## Step 8

Ask:

"Did you like this recommendation?"

If YES

Save user preference.

If NO

Recommend another meal.
